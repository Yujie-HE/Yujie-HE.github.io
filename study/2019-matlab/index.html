TJ-100177 MATLAB及其工程应用 | MATLAB and Its Applications in Engineering (Elective Course)

Course info

Supervised by Professor, Yiru Dai.

There are six assignments as of 19.06.19. The course covers the following topics:

  • Matlab basic operations
  • Numeral and Symbolic calculations
  • Function and plot basics
  • Simulink basics
  • Stateflow basics
  • Optimization toolbox

Weekly solution and codes are available in . md. If you are used to using .html or .doc(x), you can open the corresponding file.

Best wishes!
